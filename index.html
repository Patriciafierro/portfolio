Introduction

I'm Patricia Fierro, a graphic and web designer based in Buenos Aires, Argentina. With over 15 years of experience in visual design, I have worked with brands in Venezuela and Argentina, creating pieces that communicate clearly and generate impact.

My expertise spans from developing complete visual identities and editorial materials to designing web experiences, social media content, and audiovisual production. I combine technical precision with a creative sensibility built across agencies, studios, and independent work.

I work remotely with international clients, adapting each project to the brand's goals and market needs.
